Writer's Chat Chronicler (Auto-save for ChatGPT & Gemini)
Writer’s Chat Chronicler is an MVP of a local archiver. It is designed for authors, screenwriters, and researchers who need a simple way to save their creative dialogues from ChatGPT and Gemini.
It will add small button to your browser, where you can turn auto-saving chats on\off (saves in .txt to your pc).

Concept & Purpose
The primary goal of this tool is to provide independent backup of your creative dialogues. 
This userscript automatically save and organize conversation history (text chat) from AI platforms (ChatGPT and Gemini).
It's a "safety net" to ensure your work stays with you, even if your account will be blocked or your VPN \ internet connection will fail: you will still have your chat saved.
When you discuss something like book plot or long research, or just give to AI your thoughts, but you fear that this chat can be accidently deleted or you will loose access (subscription will end, or you will be banned, 
or your VPN will fail and you won’t get access anymore), you just turn AUTOSAVE button - and script will save all messages history (your chat with AI) in .txt which you can use for futher reference.
It is tailored for:
Authors & Enthusiasts: To keep track of plot ideas and character drafts.
Curious Researchers: To maintain a simple record of AI interactions.
MVP Users: Anyone who wants a local copy of their chats without complex setups.

Features (Experimental)
Basic Archiving: Saves your chats to prevent data loss during long sessions.
Local Backup: Creates a copy of your work that doesn't rely on cloud history.
Multi-Platform: Supports ChatGPT and Google Gemini.
Simple Versioning: Basic tracking of different versions of your creative output.

Installation
To use this script, you need a userscript manager installed in your browser:
Install Tampermonkey or Violentmonkey from your browser's extension store.
Create a new script in the manager.
Copy the code from script.user.js in this repository and paste it into the editor.
Save the script and refresh your AI chat pages.

How to Use
Once installed, the script adds the "Chronicler" interface to your chat pages.
Archive Toggle: Enable/disable saving.
Deep Scan: Manually trigger a capture of the thread.
Version Control: Access previously saved versions of the dialogue.
